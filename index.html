FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How does the Sprynt kiosk work?

Simply scan the QR code at the kiosk, upload your file or photo, pay via UPI, and collect your print — all in under 60 seconds. No app or account needed.

Q2. What if my print gets stuck or fails mid-way?

Don't worry — if your print fails or gets stuck, your payment is automatically refunded to your UPI account within 2–4 Hours. Just tap 'Report an Issue' on the kiosk screen or contact our support.

Q5. Is my payment and data secure?

Absolutely. Every session is fully encrypted and automatically cleared after your print. We never store your files, photos or payment details on the kiosk.

Q3. Which file formats are supported?

prynt supports PDF, JPG, PNG and Word documents. Simply upload from your phone's gallery or Google Drive directly through the kiosk screen.
